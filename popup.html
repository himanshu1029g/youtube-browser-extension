YT Study Helper

Smart YouTube controls for studying

Features
⏸ Auto Pause on Tab Switch
Pauses lecture when you leave the tab. Resumes when you come back.
🎵 Dual Tab Sync
Lecture pauses → music plays. Music pauses → lecture resumes.
Ctrl ⇪
Capture only the video frame → copies to clipboard. Just Ctrl+V to paste in Word.
